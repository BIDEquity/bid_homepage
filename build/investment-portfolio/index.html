Investment Portfolio

Our portfolio consists of several investment platforms in attractive, growing markets. Each platform has a distinct strategy to become a leading group in its respective market, both through organic growth and by acquiring synergistic new companies. We actively contribute our broad experience as well as a standardized set of value levers for software companies to fast-track sales growth, efficiency in development and delivery, and cloud transitions. As a result, most of our groups have significantly grown since the initial anchor investment was made.

myneva Group

The myneva Group is one of the leading European providers of software for social care segments such as elderly care, social care, and youth shelters. The group combines the specific market expertise of several companies in order to create a more lively and human interaction between caretakers and caregivers. Large social enterprises with activities across these segments benefit from a vendor that can deliver more integrated, connected solutions. As of today, more than 100.000 users work with the group’s software solutions daily across several European countries.

Key facts:

  • ​Markets: Elderly, social, youth, disabled, clinics
  • Geography: Germany, BeNeLux, Austria, Switzerland
  • Initial investment: 2017

SalesTech Group

For more than 25 years, customers of Infopark and PiSA sales have been relying on a tailor-made software for . From strategy to implementation and operation – everything from one source. Infopark offers modern frameworks and proven web services for the Internet and intranet. The manufacturer of the web-based Content Management Software Scrivito, which is used by well-known companies and public authorities for the creation of particularly secure and maintenance-free websites, web applications, portals and apps, joins forces with PiSA sales. The software solutions of PiSA are used by more than 500 medium-sized and large industrial customers and represent strategic business applications for sales, marketing and customer service. The group employs over 120 people in Berlin and Wroclaw (Poland).

Key facts:

  • Markets: Sales Software (CMS/ CRM/ CPQ)
  • Geography: Germany, Poland & global sales
  • Initial investment: 2020

XClinical

XClinical is a leading international provider of software solutions for drug and medical device studies. The XClinical platform fully implements the CDISC standard, which significantly accelerates the analysis and submission of data to regulatory authorities. In addition to data management, it also includes randomization, study equipment management, patient surveys, coding, and reporting. XClinical is characterized by a high degree of flexibility and short response times, and optimally meets the requirements of its customers. Nearly 50 employees in Munich, Basel, Nantes, and New Jersey support the software and its customers all over the world.

Key facts:

  • Markets: Drug and medical device studies
  • Geography: Germany, Switzerland, France, USA + global sales
  • Initial investment: 2019

Midoco Group

The Midoco Group is the market-leading provider of mid-office software for all segments of travel distribution in the DACH region. Midoco’s cloud platform automates core processes of travel booking and management and is used by online and offline travel agencies as well as in-house travel agencies for tourism and business travel. Midoco increases the degree of automation in the travel industry, which is characterized by the breaking up of existing value chains and system landscapes.

Key facts:

  • Markets: Travelindustry
  • Geography: Germany, Switzerlandv + global sales
  • Initial investment: 2016

eSight Energy Group

eSight Energy provides a unique energy management platform that enables industrial and real estate clients to optimize the energy consumption of their facilities and buildings. With offices in the USA, Europe and Asia, eSight currently serves customers in 60 countries and 17 languages. The company’s software solutions offer a variety of features that are highly valued by large corporations, public institutions, and upper midsize companies. In addition to its global reach, eSight differentiates itself through a mature product that is optimized for use in large-scale projects with millions of data records.

Key facts:

  • ​Markets: Energy management
  • Geography: UK, USA, China & global sales
  • Initial investment: 2018

ABIONIK Group

ABIONIK is a global group with a passion for clean water and air. The vision is to make life more worthwhile by providing technology for the end2end water and air purification value chain. Therefore this focus addresses a global need and mega-trend, which accelerates particularly in emerging regions such as China and India. The groups brand values are rooted in state-of-the-art German engineering, providing more efficient and cost-effective equipment to industrial and governmental clients alike. Furthermore, the group produces autonomous drinking water units for remote regions and crisis situations.

Key facts:

  • Markets: Waste water/air treatment, drinking water
  • Geography: Germany, China, India + global sales
  • Initial investment: 2017

Aeotec Group

Aeotec is a leading manufacturer of Smart Home products. Based on a broad portfolio of high-quality actuators, sensors and control units, the Aeotec Group serves applications from large-scale commercial projects, special developments and consumer applications. The main application is Z-Wave radio technology, which enables devices such as luminaires, thermostats and locking mechanisms to communicate energy-efficiently in closed and open rooms. It thus forms the backbone for a home automation network. The group is active worldwide and primarily serves American and European projects and retailers.

Key facts:

  • Markets: Smart home
  • Geography: China, Germany & global sales
  • Initial investment: 2016
